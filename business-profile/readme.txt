== Description ==

Enter and display your business contact details in Schema.org markup. You can add a Google Map, a Google "get directions" link, opening hours, contact phone number and email address, and more.

This plugin was designed for small businesses with a single business location.

== Installation ==

1. Unzip `business-profile.zip`
2. Upload the contents of `business-profile.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. Go to the Business Profile page in your admin menu. You will find it near the bottom.
